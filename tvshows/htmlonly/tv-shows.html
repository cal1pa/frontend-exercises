Shameless

Description

Shameless is an American comedy-drama television series developed by John Wells which debuted on Showtime on January 9, 2011. It is an adaptation of Paul Abbott's British series of the same name and features an ensemble cast led by William H. Macy and Emmy Rossum. The show is set on the South Side of Chicago and depicts the dysfunctional family of Frank Gallagher, a single father of six children. While he spends his days drunk, his kids learn to take care of themselves.

Main Characters

  • Frank Gallagher - played by William H. Macy
  • Fiona Gallagher - played by Emmy Rossum
  • Lip Gallagher - played by Jeremy Allen White
  • Ian Gallagher - played by Cameron Monaghan
  • Debbie Gallagher - played by Emma Kenney
  • Carl Gallagher - played by Ethan Cutkosky
  • Liam Gallagher - played by Christian Isaiah (seasons 8-11), others previously
